WHO IS HIKEHOST

New York State Forest Preserve blankets a little more than 2.6 million acres of our beautiful state, most of which average people don’t even know exists. HikeHost was founded in 2015 by Marc Anderson to bring those forests closer to reality; to introduce everyday working people to the world of wilderness exploration and the wonderful outdoor culture that exists beyond our cities.

When you hike with us in one of New York’s beautiful wild forests, you join a loving family of likeminded individuals whose sole purpose is to enjoy the outdoors. We are a kind, and respectful community that is always looking for new friends and family to bring into our wonderful world.

Join our family today and let one of HikeHost’s NYS Certified Wilderness Guides show you the way to some of this nation’s most beautiful natural treasures.

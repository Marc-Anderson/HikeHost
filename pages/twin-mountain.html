Twin Mountain

Aptly named for its perfectly shaped, dual peaks, Twin Mountain offers excellent nature walks, and the most beautiful views the Indian Head Wilderness has to offer. Whether you fancy a leisurely trek through a beautiful forest or a moderate hike to an expansive view, Twin Mountain is the perfect getaway. You don’t have to be an experienced hiker to see this spectacular summit; with HikeHost anyone with a little motivation can safely make it to this mind-blowing overlook.
